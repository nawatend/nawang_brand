Parkspot (In Progress)
Parking in city centers is a big problem. Car drivers lack accurate information of free parking places. Cars are driving around unnecessarily to find a parking place. This results in extra traffic and CO2 in the city.

New computer vision technology improved in the last few years and enables accurate parking information today. Also the performance of small hardware CPU has increased over the last years and will continue increasing. This enables low costs solutions so that there are no financial limits to new parking information solutions.

Improve traffic flows in city centers by better parking advice to drivers
Javascript, React Native, React, HTML, SASS, Mongo, Node.js, Express [MERN]
